Installation

Here are the things you need to know.

What are you saving financially?

We use independent solar software to get this done, depending on your location and the availability of this feature in your location.

Do I need to get planning permission?

Planning permission is usually needed if the installation is ground mounted, in a conservation area, government laid our roots or a government listed building.

Hidden costs?

When we send a quote to you, be rest assured that there would not be any hidden costs, for supplies and installation of panels, inverter, certificate of completion, or any other related charges, so everything you need to get up and running is included.

Quote Validity.

Our quotes are valid for a duration fo 30 days, from the date it was sent to you. If the quote exceeds this duration without any activity, we are happy to prepare a new quote for you and always ready to serve your needs.

Where do the cables & inverter go?

Cables will need to be fed from the PV Panels to the inverter and from the inverter to your consumer unit (fuse board) and batteries will also need to be connected to your consumer unit.

How do I know how much of my Solar PV electric I’m using?

There are a variety of monitors, indicators available. Please speak to the site surveyor or call the office and we’ll be able to advise on the latest devices available.

I’m ready to go Naked!

All you need at this point will be for you to give us a call, we usually set up a free site visitation, or to book your installation slot, we would then ask for 80% to be paid not less than 48 hours before the date of installation to enable us get all your parts for installation ready, after installation is complete, you then make the final 20% payment.

How long does installation take?

Most domestic installations can be completed in one day. We’ll let you know if we expect yours to take longer. Our weekly domestic schedule has installations on Monday – Thursday and we always keep Friday free to catch up with anything that was left over from the week.

After service follow up

Well after all of this, we would send you a mail or schedule a call after one month to make sure you are happy and enjoying your system, we will also contact you from time to time, usually yearly to check and remind you of any maintenance if applicable to you type of system.
